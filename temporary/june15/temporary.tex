\begin{mydef}[Relevancy]\label{mydef:relevancy}
Let $\pi$ be a feasible error trace and let $\pi[i]$ be an assignment statement of the form $x:=expr$. We call the statement $\pi[i]$ \textbf{responsible for the error} if there exists some mapping $f(y,z,..)$ such that the trace $\pi'$ that is obtained from $\pi$ by replacing $\pi[i]$ by $x:=f(y,z...)$ is infeasible.
\end{mydef}
